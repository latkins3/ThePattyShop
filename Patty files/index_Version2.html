A local hidden gem with real history

Serving the community since 1979, The Patty Shop is the kind of place people remember — and keep coming back to.

Made for quick comfort

We’re a takeout-only neighbourhood bakery built around the classics: fresh patties with flaky crusts, comforting fillings, and baked treats that feel like home. It’s warm, affordable food you can grab and go — perfect for a lunch break, a snack, or a “bring some home” kind of stop.

Rooted in Vancouver

Founded in 1979, The Patty Shop has been part of the West Side’s everyday rhythm for decades. Customers have been stopping in for years — some for the first time, others for the hundredth — all for that unmistakable smell of a bakery that’s been doing it right for a long time.

Simple, friendly, and worth the trip

No fuss, no trend-chasing — just honest Caribbean-inspired bakery comfort at a great price. If you’re looking for a spot that feels authentic, welcoming, and truly local, you’ve found it.
